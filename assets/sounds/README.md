# Sound Assets

Place your sound files in this directory:

## Required Files:

1. **background_music.wav** - Background music (scientific, no vocals)
   - Should be a looping track (will loop automatically)
   - Can be short (16+ seconds) - the app will loop it seamlessly

2. **touch_sound.wav** - Touch/click sound effect
   - Short sound (50-200ms)
   - Should be subtle and not annoying when repeated

## File Format:
- Format: WAV (currently used) or MP3
- Sample rate: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- Bitrate: 128-192 kbps for music, 64-128 kbps for sound effects

## Where to Get Free Assets:

1. **Freesound.org** - https://freesound.org
   - Search for "scientific ambient" or "laboratory background"
   - Search for "ui click" or "button tap" for touch sounds
   - Filter by Creative Commons licenses

2. **OpenGameArt.org** - https://opengameart.org
   - Browse music section for ambient/scientific tracks
   - Browse sound effects for UI sounds

3. **Zapsplat.com** - https://www.zapsplat.com
   - Free account required
   - Good selection of UI sounds and ambient music

4. **Incompetech.com** - https://incompetech.com/music/
   - Kevin MacLeod's royalty-free music
   - Search for "ambient" or "scientific" themes

5. **Pixabay.com** - https://pixabay.com/music/
   - Free music and sound effects
   - Good for ambient/scientific tracks

## Example Search Terms:
- Background music: "scientific ambient", "laboratory music", "space ambient", "electronic ambient"
- Touch sound: "ui click", "button tap", "soft click", "gentle tap"
